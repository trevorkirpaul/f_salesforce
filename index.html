The Art of Development

a blog from the perspective of a new web developer

Sunday, April 22nd, 2018

I've been working as a Web Developer since mid-Febuary. I'm currently on two projects, both using React Native. One of the projects I'm on has to do with SalesForce but I honestly have nothing against it.

Getting this job was a huge career and lifestyle change for me. I was previously in the service industry as a bar tender but I always knew it was a temporary gig.

It's been over 2 months now and I still can't believe that this is my life. I truly get to do something I enjoy and get paid for it. I will have to say that the transition has been tough. I've been very busy and I feel like I spend all my time working. Whiles I'm at work I focus in on coding for either of these two projects and this even extends to when I'm home.

From what I had previously read, before getting this job, a lot of people wouldn't put up with this. I can personally deal with it all because I know that I'm very new at web development and it will definitely take extra time for me to get the work done.

I've learned so much and there isn't a single job out there that I'd rather do. It's been very rewarding and I'm always excited to wake up on a week day.

That's basically my life right now. This blog will focus on the small details and my experience at work and at home revolving around coding, web development and my personal growth.

I'm currently trying to figure out how I'll make this blog, I'm leaning towards RoR with a React front end. I'm definitely going to add in markdown support as well. This will be a great way for me to keep track of this journey and maybe someone will find it entertaining.

Hopefully it helps my writing skills as well!
